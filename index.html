Tom Yum Goong:A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and authentic spices. This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing lemongrass,galangal and kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp.

"The key to a delicous Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors-sweet,sour,salty and spicy"

Chef Somchai

Ingredients

  • Lemongrass:2 stalks
  • Thai Basil;1 cup
  • Kaffir lime leaves:3 leaves
  • Shrimp:500g

Preparations:

  1. Simmer the broth for 10minutes
  2. Add lemongrass,galangal,and kaffir lime leaves
  3. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink
  4. Season with fish sauce,lime juice and chili paste
  5. Garnish with Thia Basil and serve hot

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4cup of fish sauce adds authentic flavor
  • 1Tablespoon of chili paste gives it signature heat
